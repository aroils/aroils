Groundnut Oil

Groundnut oil, also known as peanut oil, is a popular cooking oil derived from peanuts. It has a high smoke point and a mild flavor, making it ideal for frying and sautéing. Rich in monounsaturated fats, it is considered a healthier option compared to some other oils.

About Us

Established in 1967 by A. Ranganathan, our family-run business has proudly upheld the tradition of producing high-quality edible oils for over five decades. Using the age-old wood press (chekku) method, we extract groundnut oil, sesame oil, and coconut oil, preserving their natural aroma, flavor, and nutritional value. What began as a humble endeavor by Mr. Ranganathan has now been carried forward by his children with the same dedication, authenticity, and commitment to purity. Today, we continue to blend tradition with trust, delivering wholesome oils that generations have relied on. AR OILS is dedicated to providing high-quality cooking oils sourced from the finest ingredients. Our mission is to promote healthy cooking and enhance culinary experiences with our premium oils.
